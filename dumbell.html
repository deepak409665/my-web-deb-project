Dumbbell Workouts at Home

Chest Workout: Use dumbbells for bench presses and flys to build chest strength.
Bicep Workout: Perform curls and hammer curls to tone your biceps effectively.
Leg Workout: Incorporate squats and lunges with dumbbells for strong legs.
